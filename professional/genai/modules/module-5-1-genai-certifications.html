The financial aid route — getting paid certificates free

All Coursera certifications offer financial aid. Most Indian students do not know how easy the application is.

Process: Open any Coursera course → Click "Enroll for Free" → Scroll down → Click "Financial Aid available" → Fill a 200-word application explaining your situation → Submit. Approval rate is approximately 80% within 15 days. The application is evaluated honestly — if you genuinely cannot afford the certificate, you will likely be approved. What to write: Be specific and honest. "I am a final-year BTech student in [city]. The course certificate will help me in campus placements at companies like TCS and Infosys. I cannot currently afford the fee because [reason]." 200 words is enough.

This route gives you the same certificate as anyone who paid full price. No marking of financial aid on the certificate — it looks identical.
